Tokenomics

The $MUSKVISION Token

A BEP20 token launched on Binance Smart Chain network on 18th september, 2021.

Auto Rewards

4% of every transaction with $MUSKVISION is deducted and redistributed to all our token holders. If you are holding $MUSKVISION, you will see your $MUSKVISION token getting increased automatically as the community transacts.

Auto Burn

Roughly 1.1% of every transaction is sent to BSC burn address, therefore taking it out of circulation. We have already started with taking 25% of our total token supply out of circulation. $MUSKVISION value increases with time.

Auto Liquidity

4% of every transaction with $MUSKVISION is automatically added to $MUSKVISION-$BNB liquidity pool on PancakeSwap. 2% of the amount goes to $MUSKVISION and other 2% goes to $BNB. This helps in maintaining balance and stability of token price.

How To Buy

It's just 3 easy steps. Buy and hold for a better future.

SETUP

  • Setup a secure crypto wallet of your choice, if you don't have it already.
  • Make sure it is compatible with PancakeSwap exchange. MetaMask, TrustWallet, Binance Chain Wallet are commonly used.
  • Add BSC Mainnet to the network list and connect to PancakeSwap. For more info click here.

BUY BNB

  • Buy Binance coin (BNB) from your preferred exchanges such as Binance, Coinbase, WazirX, etc.
  • Transfer it to your crypto wallet (MetaMask, etc.) using BEP20 network i.e. Binance Smart Chain.
  • Transfers made to wrong network cannot be reverted. Please be careful.

SWAP

  • Connect your crypto wallet to PancakeSwap.
  • Go to exchange tab and exchange your $BNB with $MUSKVISION.
  • Copy and paste the $MUSKVISION contract address to add it manually in case it is not appearing.
  • Set the slippage to 10-12% to avoid transaction failures. BUY NOW

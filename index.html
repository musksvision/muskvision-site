Tokenomics

Rewards

4% of every transaction with $MUSKVISION is deducted and redistributed to all our token holders. If you are holding $MUSKVISION, you will see your $MUSKVISION token getting increased automatically as the community transacts.

Auto Burn

There is an automatic burn feature in $MUSKVISION. Roughly 2% of the amount of all transactions are burnt to BSC burn address. We have already started with 40% of the supply burned. $MUSK VISION value increases with time.

Auto Liquidity

4% of every transaction with $MUSKVISION is automatically added to $MUSKVISION-$BNB liquidity pool on PancakeSwap. 2% of the amount goes to $MUSKVISION and other 2% goes to $BNB. This helps in maintaining balance and stability of token price.

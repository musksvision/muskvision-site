Tokenomics

REWARDS

4% of every transaction with $MUSK VISION is deducted and redistributed to all our token holders. If you are holding $MUSK VISION you will see your wallet getting increased with $MUSK VISION token automatically as the community transacts.

BURN

There is an automatic burn feature in $MUSK VISION. Roughly 2% of the amount of all transactions are burnt to BSC burn address. We have already started with 40% of the supply burned. $MUSK VISION value increases with time.

LIQUIDITY

4% of every transaction with $MUSK VISION is added to MUSKVISION-BNB liquidity on PancakSwap. 2% of the amount goes to $MUSK VISION and other 2% goes to $BNB. This helps in balance and stability of token price.

TOKEN TRUST

We are a community token. To build the trust within the community, we will be additionally adding 1% of total available supply in liquidity to PancakeSwap at regular intervals. Proofs will shared on the website and other social platforms. By doing this, we will be preventing token from sudden tanking or surge. Also making it rug & pull proof.
